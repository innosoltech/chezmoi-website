About Chez Moi

Located at one of Ghana's prime location, Spintex, in the capital city, Accra, Chez Moi is just about a 15 minutes drive away from Kotoka International Airport. The hotel happens to be located in the same community as some of Ghana's largest shopping mall chains - China Mall, Max Mart, Orca Deco, Melcom, etc. Therefore providing a convenient stay for guest that like to go shopping. For guest that like to try out dishes, Chez Moi also happens to find itself in the same cummunity as some of Ghana's finest restaurants, serving local & continental cuisine (Chinese, Indian, Turkish, and more). By virtue of its location, getting around the city is relatively easier.
